Moral Waiver Determination App

Regulation-strict mode using AR 601-210 Chapter 4 as the decision baseline and your uploaded waiver checklist/TTP files only for packet-building steps. This version uses charge-by-charge disposition handling, excludes dismissed charges from threshold counts, stops processing on pending charges, and uses the original Army Waiver Packet Builder look and flow. It also includes PS/NPS selection and explicitly labels 4-7c nonwaiverable triggers so the output shows whether a major misconduct case stays waiver-eligible or becomes nonwaiverable.
